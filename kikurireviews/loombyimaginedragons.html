Quickie Review

review

this was torture. let's ignore the terrible sex joke I made.

>Wake Up. 3 overproduced hype song.

Nice to Meet You. 2.5 annoying, overproduced, the sample is headaching.

Eyes Closed. 3 production isn't great, but it has interesting moments. "Among Us" got me.

Take Me to the Beach. 3 vocals are overly produced to the point it's annoying, boring.

In Your Corner. 4 production is fin-ish, however its incredibly boring with no interesting moments.

Gods Don't Pray. 2 poorly paced, overproduced, overproduced vocals.

Don't Forget Me. 4.

Kid. 1. the worst song I've heard all year holy shit. this is one of the worst produced songs I've ever heard in my life. Trees (Regional at Best version) and Never Take It (Scaled and Icy studio version) by twenty one pilots are better produced, and that's saying something.

Fire in These Hills. 4 it exists.

Nice to Meet You (feat: J Balvin). 2.5 production isn't great, but it has interesting moments. "Among Us" got me like always. J Balvin has no reason to be here.
